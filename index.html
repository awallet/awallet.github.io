Who was I ?

One-liner

Researcher in Crypto and Maths.


Detailed

I am a full-time researcher at Inria Rennes, within IRISA. In general, I like about everything summed up by the following keywords:

  • Algorithms, Cryptology, Computer Security
  • Algebraic Geometry, Number Theory, Computer Algebra

More precisely, at the moment I am interested in lattice-based cryptography. My work deals with the study of its theoretical foundations, as well as concrete and practical realizations of constructions and attacks.

Previously, I was a post-doctorate researcher in NTT Secure Platform Laboratories, in Tokyo. I worked there with Mehdi Tibouchi. Before that, I was a post-doctorate researcher in team AriC, at LIP, in Ecole Normale Supérieure de Lyon (ENSL), France. I worked there with Damien Stehlé.
