Detailed

I am a post-doctorate researcher in team AriC, at LIP, in Ecole Normale Supérieure de Lyon (ENSL). My current research interests are the mathematical foundations of (post-quantum) cryptography, with an emphasis on lattice-based cryptography. I am working under the supervision of Damien Stehlé.

In general, I like about everything summed up by the following keywords:

  • Algorithms, Cryptology, Computer Security
  • Algebraic Geometry, Number Theory, Computer Algebra

Previously, I was a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Université Pierre et Marie Curie (UPMC, Paris) and member of team PolSyS, in LIP6, under the supervision of Jean-Charles Faugère and Vanessa Vitse. My thesis subject was the Discrete Logarithm Problem in the divisor class group of algebraic curves (read more here).
